Ryans Famous Spaghetti Receipe


Description

This is the famous dish by non other than Ryan Kreps. A healthy choice for all healthy food lovers that also love carbs :)

Serves 8 to 10 people

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Whole Wheat Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 pound of ground deer meat (or meat of your choice)
  • 15 Kalamata olives
  • 1 can of Marinara Sauce
  • 1 TBS Himylayan Salt
  • 1 TBS Pepper
  • 1/2 TBS Cyanne Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Deer meat over stove at medium heat. Once cooked add salt, pepper, and Cyanne pepper
  2. Add Marinara and Kalamata olives to meat in pan. Turn pan to low heat and add lid
  3. Boil Water to rolling boil and add appropriate amount of spaghetti. Do not break spaghetti in half. VERY IMPORTANT
  4. Add Spaghetti to meat sauce, stir and let sit for an additional 10 minutes to marinate.
