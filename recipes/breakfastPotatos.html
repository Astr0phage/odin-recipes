Ryan's Perfect Health Breakfast Potatoes


Description

This is the famous dish by non other than Ryan Kreps. A healthy choice for all healthy food lovers that also love carbs :) Below will show you how to make the perfect breakfast potatos to pair with your Perfect Eggs

Serves 2 to 4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 LB Small Potatos
  • 1 TBS Vegatble oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into small squares no bigger than 1 inch
  2. Heat pan to medium/high heat and add Oil
  3. Once oil is hot put one peice of potato in the oil, if it starts to sizzle its time to put the rest in
  4. Cover lid for 5 minutes
  5. Remove lid and Stir potatoes. Put lid back on for 3 minutes.
  6. Remove lid and Stir potatoes. Put lid back on for 3 minutes.
  7. Remove lid, stir and check for desired browness
  8. Once you have desired browness add to plate and pair with Ryan's Perfect Eggs
