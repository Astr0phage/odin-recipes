Ryan's Perfect Eggs


Description

This is the famous dish by non other than Ryan Kreps. A healthy choice for all healthy food lovers that also love carbs :) Below will show you how to make the perfect eggs for breakfast

Serves 1 to 2 people

Ingredients

  • 2 Farm Raised Eggs
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Pepper
  • TBS of Milk

Steps

  1. Turn Stove on Low Heat
  2. Crack eggs and add them to a bowl with Milk and stir with fork (do not blend with blender)
  3. Add Eggs to pan and let cook mixing every 20 to 40 seconds. The slower your eggs cook the better they will be
  4. Once they are to desired fluffyness add to plate
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Put those eggs into your mouth :)
